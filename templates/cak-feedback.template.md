# CAK Feedback Log -- <project-name>

One line per kit interaction worth remembering. No structure required
beyond the bullet points. Be honest, not generous.

## How to log

- Add a dated line whenever the kit helped you, annoyed you, gave wrong
  advice, or did nothing when it should have.
- Keep entries short. Three to six words is enough most of the time.
- Tag with one of: [help] [noise] [wrong] [missing] [bug]
